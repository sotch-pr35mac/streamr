Welcome to streamr!

Streamr gives you the ability to read through massive amounts of text quickly and easily using revolutionary Spritz and OCR technology.

Built by students, for students. Streamr is ideal for students with heavy reading list who are looking for a fast and efficient way to comprehend their reading.
