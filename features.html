Features

Increase your reading speed and your comprehension.

Studies have shown that Streamr can increase your reading comprehension while increasing your speed dramatically.

Built for students.

Streamr was built for students, by students. This service can be used to read large quantities of information for classes, proving to be a valuable study tool.

Streamr is built by the community.

Streamr would not be possible if it were not for Miserlou's OpenSpritz and antimatter15's OCRAD.js.
